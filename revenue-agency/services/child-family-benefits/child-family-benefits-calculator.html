What you can calculate

The calculation includes the following child and family benefits:

  • Canada child benefit (including related provincial and territorial programs)
  • Goods and services tax/harmonized sales tax credit (including related provincial and territorial programs)
  • Advanced Canada workers benefit
  • Canada Carbon Rebate
  • Ontario senior homeowners' property tax grant
  • Ontario trillium benefit (includes the Ontario sales tax credit, Ontario energy and property tax credit, and Northern Ontario energy credit)

The calculation does not include amounts from the:

  • Canada Dental Benefit
