Payment dates

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) sends monthly payments for the Canada Child Benefit (CCB).

On this page

  • Payment dates for the CCB
  • Check the status of your payments
  • When to expect your first payment

Payment dates for the CCB

You should receive your CCB payments by the middle of each month. You can sign up for direct deposit if you do not already have it set up with the CRA. Direct deposit is the fastest and most secure way to get your benefit payments.

Dates for payments made in 2025

Your benefit payments are recalculated every July based on your adjusted family net income tax information from the previous year. Each 12-month payment period begins on July 1 and ends on June 30 of the following year.

Check the status of your payments

If you want to receive email notifications about upcoming benefit payments, you can sign up to receive benefit and credit payment reminders. The reminders are sent about one week before the payment is issued.

If you have a CRA account

You can sign in to your CRA account to view:

  • Your next expected payment date and amount
  • The status and amount of each payment for the benefit year, from July to the following June
  • Your statement of account for the CCB, including details about amounts and entitlements, dates, and balances

CRA sign in Register for a CRA account

  1. Choose your Individual account
  2. On the Overview screen, find your next expected payment under Benefits and credits
  3. Select Benefits and credits to find detailed payment information
  4. Go to Canada Child Benefit (CCB) to find your next expected payment date and amount, payment schedule, and your statement of account

If you have not received your payment

You will not receive a monthly payment if your total benefit amount for the year is less than $240. Instead you will receive one lump sum payment with your July payment.

If you don't receive your CCB payment on the expected payment date, before you contact us, you can:

  • Check the status of your payment in your CRA account
  • Make sure your personal information is up to date
  • Check other reasons for stopped or changed payments

If you still don't know why you haven't received your payment, wait 5 business days after your expected payment date before you contact us.

When to expect your first payment

You will receive your first CCB payment after the CRA has determined your eligibility and processed your application.

Check the status of your application

You may be able to check the status of your application in your CRA accountusing the progress tracker.

Processing times for your CCB application will vary based on how you applied.

  1. Choose the option you used to apply
  2. Select the Show targeted processing times button to find the current processing time
