Contact the CRA

If your information has changed, you can complete most of your updates online with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). Using your CRA account to update your information is the easiest and fastest way to provide changes to us.

If there is an issue with your payments, you can find some of the reasons for stopped or changed payments before contacting us.

Online:

Get your personalized benefit information in My Account.

CRA sign in Register for a CRA account

Alternative: MyCRA web app

What you can do online:

  • apply for the Canada child benefit (CCB)
  • view CCB and related provincial and territorial programs payments, account balance, and statement of account
  • view and update children in your care
  • update your personal information, including your address and your marital status
By phone:
Call CCB and related provincial and territorial programs
Before you call

To verify your identity, you'll need

  • Social Insurance Number
  • Personal details about the children in your care
  • Information from previous benefit payments

If you are calling the CRA on behalf of someone else, you must be an authorized representative.
